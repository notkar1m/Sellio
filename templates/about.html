About Sellio



Sellio is the place where you can either buy or sell a PC component. After creating your account, you can create a new listing by clicking the "New Listing" button then filling in all the fields.

But if you are looking to buy a PC component to add to your own PC, then you can either search for what you want using the search bar in the Home page or you can choose a category then find what you're looking for.

After finding what you were looking for, you can message the seller and arrange a time to meet up so they can give you the item.

If you're a seller, make sure to check your Chat Inbox every once in a while to see if anyone messaged you about your listing.

You can also favorite listings that you like can comeback to them at any time in the Favorites page.

To get more people to look at your listing, consider sharing it using the " Share" button on your listing's page. Copy the link then send it you all your friends so they can look at your listing.
You can also share someone else's listing with your friend if you think they'll need it.

If you think a listing is violating the rules or has something wrong, you can report it using the " Report" button, then specify exactly what you think is wrong with it
and we'll review your report and if it's valid we will remove the listing.

After creating a listing, if you think you made a mistake or if you already sold it, you can remove it by pressing
the " Delete" button.

The name "Sellio" originated from 2 words which are Sell and IO which stands for "Input/Output". So basically it's where you sell things with inputs and outputs.
